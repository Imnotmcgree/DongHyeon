MADE FOR STREET

Launched by NIGO® in 2010,Human Made is a Tokyo-base Lifestyle brand with the concept of “the future is in the past”. Human Made draws inspiration from a diverse mix of styles and vintage items, which embody the atmosphere of times past, while exploring bold ideas that stand out amid the constant stream of street culture.
